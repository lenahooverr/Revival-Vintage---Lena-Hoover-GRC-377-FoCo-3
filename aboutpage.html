Based in North County San Diego, Revival Vintage aims to promote shopping sustainably and locally.

The inspiration behind Revival Vintage is to motivate clientele to care for the environment by avoiding fast fashion and unethical fashion corporations that make extensive use of plastics (rather than biodegradable materials) and have a record of workers rights violations.

We Buy Vintage! Arrange an appointment to sell your clothes or artifacts. Recieve 50% Store Credit or 30% Cash Back.
